English 6X

W4

The ball flying through the air

The players stopping in awe

The goalie leaping up to stop the ball

It was a shot from the midfield line.

In the corner pocket

Across half the field

On the left, the goalie

On the right, the ball

Into the net.



Playing tennis

sun’s a menace.

Playing soccer

sun’s a shocker.

Running track

feel the heat on your back.

Swimming in the pool

finally cool.



Outside, in the spring

Wind blowing

Flowers sprouting

People playing

Balls bouncing

Sun shining.



Soccer:

It’s exciting.

To run up the field

To kick the ball into the net

To run back down

To swerve across

This is soccer.
